Academic Planning at WSU

The Academic Planning Directorate plays a central role in shaping and sustaining the academic enterprise at Walter Sisulu University. Its mandate is to ensure that the academic environment is strategically aligned, regulatorily compliant, and dynamically responsive to national higher education requirements.

Through collaboration with faculties, directorates, and external bodies such as DHET, CHE, and SAQA, the Directorate oversees programme development, accreditation, academic service delivery models, and institutional academic planning processes.

The Directorate also monitors educational compliance, academic integrity, notional hour norms, registration trends, and the Programme Qualification Mix (PQM) to ensure that WSUâ€™s academic offerings remain relevant, sustainable, and strategically aligned.
