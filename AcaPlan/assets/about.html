About Academic Planning

Strategic academic oversight ensuring programme quality, compliance, and institutional academic excellence

Introduction

The Academic Planning Directorate at Walter Sisulu University plays a strategic and foundational role in shaping the academic landscape of the institution. As WSU strives toward its vision of being an impactful, technology-infused African university, Academic Planning ensures that academic offerings are aligned, compliant, relevant, and capable of supporting institutional excellence.

In a rapidly evolving higher education environment, the need for effective academic planning, programme development, accreditation, and academic service delivery has never been more essential. Academic Planning provides the framework, oversight, and strategic guidance necessary for WSU to adapt, grow, and maintain academic integrity and quality.

For more information: Download the Academic Planning Overview (PDF)

The Rationale for Academic Planning

Academic Planning plays a pivotal role in supporting WSU’s strategic direction and academic mission. Its work ensures that academic programmes, planning processes, and institutional compliance efforts are grounded in national policy frameworks and aligned with university priorities. The Directorate’s significance lies in its ability to guide sustainable academic growth, quality assurance, and institutional alignment.

  • Academic Programmes as Strategic Assets: Academic Planning ensures that programmes are designed, accredited, and monitored in accordance with national regulatory bodies such as DHET, SAQA, and CHE.
  • Evidence-Based Academic Decision-Making: Through programme viability studies, registration monitoring, and compliance assessments, the Directorate provides insights that guide academic and institutional decision-making.
  • Enhancing Student Success: By ensuring programmes meet educational compliance and notional hour standards, Academic Planning supports the delivery of quality learning experiences that contribute to student success.
  • Optimizing Academic Resource Allocation: The Directorate advises on programme viability, alignment, and demand, ensuring that resources are allocated efficiently and sustainably.
  • Fostering Academic Excellence: Academic Planning contributes to curriculum quality, programme reviews, and academic integrity to ensure continuous improvement in teaching and learning.
  • Strategic Planning and Accountability: Academic Planning participates in shaping the institutional academic plan, the Programme Qualification Mix (PQM), and compliance with national regulations.
  • Institutional Collaboration: Working closely with faculties, directorates, and external stakeholders, the Directorate promotes transparency, alignment, and shared academic purpose.

Academic Planning ensures that WSU’s academic enterprise remains responsive, compliant, innovative, and strategically aligned with the needs of its students, communities, and the broader higher education sector.

Vision

To guide and support the development of a strategic, compliant, and forward-thinking academic environment that enables Walter Sisulu University to deliver high-quality, relevant, and sustainable academic programmes.

Mission

Our mission is to advance WSU’s academic objectives by ensuring effective planning, programme development, accreditation, and oversight of academic processes. We are committed to:

  • Developing and Monitoring the Academic Plan: Facilitate the creation of the academic calendar and academic delivery model for the institution.
  • Supporting Sustainable Programme Growth: Oversee the development, accreditation, and review of academic programmes aligned with institutional strategy and market demand.
  • Ensuring Academic Compliance: Monitor educational compliance, notional hours, academic integrity, and adherence to national regulations.
  • Enhancing Academic Efficiency: Provide guidance on registration quotas, admission patterns, and their impact on academic delivery.
  • Institutional Advisory: Support university committees such as SIQAC with expert academic insights to strengthen quality and compliance.
  • Leading Institutional Engagement: Represent WSU in national regulatory processes involving DHET, SAQA, CHE, and other key bodies.
  • Strengthening Academic Collaboration: Facilitate transparent communication and integrated planning across faculties and directorates.

Through these commitments, Academic Planning helps sustain academic excellence, institutional credibility, and strategic growth at Walter Sisulu University.

Values

The Academic Planning Directorate upholds the values outlined in WSU’s strategic plan—integrity, excellence, respect, and Ubuntu—and further commits to the following principles:

  • Academic Integrity: Ensuring honesty and accuracy in all academic planning, reporting, and compliance activities.
  • Quality and Rigor: Commitment to high academic standards in programme development, accreditation processes, and curriculum oversight.
  • Professional Excellence: Offering responsive, informed, and proactive academic support to the university community.
  • Collaborative Engagement: Working closely with faculties, committees, and external bodies to ensure alignment and cohesive academic planning.
  • Transparency and Accountability: Promoting openness in decision-making, monitoring, and programme review processes.
  • Continuous Improvement: Advancing a culture of ongoing evaluation, refinement, and academic innovation.

These values represent the core of Academic Planning’s commitment to supporting WSU’s academic mission and promoting a strong, future-focused academic environment.
