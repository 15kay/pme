Who We Are

Planning, Monitoring & Evaluation (PME) Directorate

The Planning, Monitoring and Evaluation (PME) Directorate serves as the central hub for institutional oversight, performance assurance, and strategic coordination across Walter Sisulu University. PME ensures that all university processes, plans, and reports align with organisational goals and national higher education mandates.

As the custodian of institutional performance data, PME oversees the development, verification, and governance of all official university content intended for public dissemination. This includes strategic documents, performance reports, monitoring outcomes, institutional announcements, and compliance-based submissions.

The Directorate collaborates closely with key functional units such as Institutional Research and Planning, Quality Management, and Academic Planning to ensure that all published information is accurate, quality assured, and institutionally approved. This structured workflow supports transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement at WSU.

PME also plays a leading role in maintaining the integrity of online information through a controlled content management process, ensuring that all updates, documents, and reports are reviewed and approved before publication on the universityâ€™s digital platforms.
