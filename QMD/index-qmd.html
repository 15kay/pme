Who We Are

Quality Management Directorate (QMD) at WSU

The Quality Management Directorate (QMD) serves as the custodian of institutional quality at Walter Sisulu University, ensuring that all academic and support functions align with national standards, CHE requirements, and WSUâ€™s Vision 2030. Its work strengthens institutional coherence, compliance, and continuous improvement across teaching and learning, research, and community engagement.

Core activities include the coordination of internal and external quality reviews, facilitation of programme and qualification evaluations, monitoring of Quality Improvement Plans (QIPs), and the implementation of policies that support continuous quality enhancement. QMD also ensures that standards, guidelines, templates, and criteria are consistently applied across faculties, departments, and support units.

The Directorate collaborates closely with other university entities, such as Institutional Research & Planning, Academic Planning, and the Directorate of Learning and Teaching, to provide evidence-based insights that support academic excellence, student success, and institutional effectiveness. Through this integrated approach, QMD embeds a culture of accountability, reflection, and improvement across the institution.

Recent quality initiatives include comprehensive programme reviews, faculty quality assessments, themed support-unit evaluations, and the rollout of WSUâ€™s integrated Quality Improvement Plans. These outputs contribute directly to the Institutional QA Dashboard and have informed strategic decisions adopted by Senate and other governance structures.
