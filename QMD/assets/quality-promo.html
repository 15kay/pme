Quality Promotion and Capacity Development

The Quality Promotion and Capacity Development (QPCD) function at WSU plays a crucial role in nurturing a strong, institution-wide quality culture. It ensures that all stakeholders, academic staff, support departments, students, and leadership,are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and support needed to understand and implement quality assurance and enhancement practices. QPCD promotes transparency, collaboration, and continuous institutional improvement.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Promoting a Quality Culture: Lead advocacy, communication, and awareness initiatives that ensure all university stakeholders understand their roles in sustaining quality and improving performance.
  • Capacity Development: Strengthen the abilities of faculties, departments, and support units through training workshops, quality conversations, seminars, and targeted development programmes that enhance quality management competency.
  • Quality Promotion Activities: Facilitate activities that reinforce the value of quality assurance, such as briefings, information-sharing sessions, and regular communication aligned with institutional goals.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Encourage participation from students, staff, industry experts, and other partners to broaden perspectives and ensure inclusive quality enhancement processes.
  • Support for Reviews and Improvement: Provide support to units in preparation for internal and external reviews, helping them reflect on strengths, identify gaps, and develop actionable improvement plans.
  • Continuous Improvement Advocacy: Promote the use of evidence, reflection, and innovation to move beyond minimum standards, ensuring WSU remains responsive, future-focused, and aligned with national and global higher education expectations.
  • Professionalisation of Quality Practice: Contribute to building a community of practice within WSU, enabling quality practitioners and leaders to engage with current best practices and emerging trends in higher education quality management.
