About Us

Welcome to the Quality Management Directorate

Introduction

At Walter Sisulu University, our vision of becoming “An impactful, technology-infused African University” is anchored in a strong culture of quality, excellence, and continuous improvement. The Quality Management Directorate (QMD) plays a central role in supporting this vision by ensuring that all academic and support functions operate in alignment with national standards, regulatory requirements, and the institution’s strategic priorities.

In a rapidly evolving higher education landscape—characterised by increasing complexity, accountability demands, and the need for responsive teaching and learning—the role of QMD has become more critical than ever. The Directorate ensures that the university maintains robust quality assurance systems, embraces continuous enhancement, and fosters a culture of reflection, innovation, and academic integrity.

For more information: Download the QMD Overview (PDF)

The Rationale for the Quality Management Directorate

The Quality Management Directorate is essential to the success of WSU’s mission, providing the structure, standards, and oversight needed to ensure institutional excellence. Its significance lies in its ability to safeguard the integrity of academic offerings, monitor performance, and guide the institution in developing mature, integrated, and coherent quality systems.

  • Promoting a Quality Culture: QMD leads the promotion of a campus-wide quality culture, ensuring that all faculties, departments, and support units understand and embrace their roles in upholding excellence.
  • Ensuring Standards & Compliance: The Directorate ensures alignment with CHE standards, HEQSF requirements, institutional policies, and national legislative frameworks, safeguarding the credibility of academic programmes and institutional operations.
  • Supporting Continuous Improvement: Through reviews, monitoring, and feedback cycles, QMD empowers units to identify areas for improvement and implement targeted interventions that elevate performance.
  • Driving Accountability: QMD ensures clear governance pathways and accountability structures that reinforce institutional effectiveness and responsible stewardship of academic quality.
  • Enhancing Learning, Teaching & Research Quality: The Directorate evaluates the effectiveness of academic programmes, modules, and faculty quality systems, enabling evidence-informed enhancement of teaching, learning, research, and community engagement.
  • Capacity Development & Quality Promotion: Through training, workshops, and quality conversations, the Directorate builds institutional capacity and encourages reflective, data-informed decision-making.
  • Integrated Quality Systems: QMD harmonises processes across the institution—academic planning, teaching & learning, quality promotion, and support services—ensuring consistency, coherence, and strategic alignment.

As WSU strengthens its position as a technology-infused African university, the Directorate remains committed to embedding quality into every process, decision, and initiative—ensuring a resilient, responsive, and future-ready institution.

Vision

To enable excellence by fostering a mature, coherent, and integrated quality management system that promotes continuous improvement, accountability, and innovation across all spheres of the university.

Mission

Our mission is to lead, coordinate, and enhance quality assurance and quality management across WSU by building sustainable systems, empowering stakeholders, and ensuring alignment with national standards and institutional strategic priorities. We are dedicated to:

  • Strengthening Quality Systems: Develop, implement, and monitor integrated quality management systems across faculties, departments, and support units.
  • Facilitating Programme & Institutional Reviews: Coordinate internal and external reviews to ensure programme relevance, compliance, and continuous improvement.
  • Supporting Quality Improvement: Guide the development, implementation, and monitoring of Quality Improvement Plans (QIPs) across all levels of the university.
  • Ensuring Policy Implementation: Promote and monitor adherence to quality-related policies, guidelines, and procedures.
  • Driving Quality Promotion: Encourage dialogue, capacity development, and training activities that strengthen the institutional quality culture.
  • Aligning with External Agencies: Support institutional engagements with CHE, SAQA, DHET, and professional bodies to ensure compliance and accreditation success.
  • Supporting Governance Structures: Inform decision-making by providing quality insights to Senate, SQAC, Council, and other governance bodies.

Through these commitments, the Directorate ensures that WSU maintains excellence in teaching, learning, research, community engagement, and administrative support.

Values

The Quality Management Directorate upholds the values enshrined in the University's strategic plan, including honesty and integrity, quality and excellence, respect, and Ubuntu. Additionally, the Directorate is guided by the following values:

  • Accountability: Encouraging responsibility and ownership of quality standards across the institution.
  • Integration: Promoting coherent, harmonized systems that connect academic and support functions.
  • Fitness for Purpose: Ensuring that programmes, services, and processes meet their intended objectives.
  • Fitness of Purpose: Aligning quality activities with institutional and national imperatives.
  • Innovation: Embracing adaptive, technology-driven approaches to enhance quality practices.
  • Simplification: Streamlining processes and reducing complexity in quality management systems.
  • Collaboration: Working jointly with faculties, departments, and support units to achieve shared quality goals.

These values represent our unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement, positioning QMD as a key driver of institutional quality at Walter Sisulu University.
