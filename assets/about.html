About PME Directorate

Planning, Monitoring & Evaluation at Walter Sisulu University

Our Purpose

The Planning, Monitoring and Evaluation (PME) Directorate serves as the strategic coordination hub for institutional oversight, performance assurance, and evidence-based decision-making across Walter Sisulu University. We ensure that all university processes, plans, and reports align with organizational goals and national higher education mandates.

Driving Excellence Through Data

We transform institutional data into actionable insights that guide strategic decisions, enhance performance, and support sustainable growth across all university operations.

As the custodian of institutional performance data, PME oversees the development, verification, and governance of all official university content intended for public dissemination. This includes strategic documents, performance reports, monitoring outcomes, and compliance-based submissions.

Our Strategic Role

PME plays a pivotal role in ensuring that planning, monitoring, and evaluation processes are effective, evidence-based, and aligned with institutional goals. Our strategic importance includes:

  • Data as a Strategic Asset: We ensure that institutional data is accurate, reliable, and transformed into actionable insights that guide decision-making and drive institutional improvements.
  • Evidence-Based Decision-Making: Through systematic monitoring and evaluation, we support leadership in making informed decisions that enhance academic and operational outcomes.
  • Performance Excellence: By tracking key performance indicators and assessing institutional effectiveness, we enable continuous improvements in planning, teaching, and administration.
  • Resource Optimization: We provide insights for efficient deployment of financial and human resources, ensuring sustainability and accountability.
  • Strategic Alignment: We monitor and evaluate the implementation of strategic plans, ensuring alignment with university objectives and fostering institutional accountability.
  • Collaborative Excellence: We facilitate interdepartmental cooperation and data transparency, ensuring cohesive and coordinated institutional actions.

Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate, timely, and actionable insights that support the university's strategic objectives through comprehensive planning, monitoring, and evaluation services. We are committed to:

  • Strategic Planning Excellence: Supporting faculties and departments in developing operational and strategic plans aligned with institutional priorities.
  • Performance Monitoring: Tracking key performance indicators and providing reports that promote accountability, transparency, and informed decision-making.
  • Student Success Enhancement: Analyzing institutional and academic data to improve student outcomes, retention, and graduation rates.
  • Resource Optimization: Guiding resource allocation through comprehensive monitoring and evaluation of programs and initiatives.
  • Quality Assurance: Monitoring adherence to institutional policies, accreditation requirements, and best practices.
  • Research Excellence: Conducting institutional research to support strategic initiatives, policy development, and operational improvements.

Our Values

The PME Directorate upholds the core values of Walter Sisulu University while emphasizing our commitment to excellence:

Data Integrity

Ensuring all institutional data is accurate, reliable, and consistent across all platforms and reports.
